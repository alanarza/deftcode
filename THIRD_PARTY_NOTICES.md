# Third-party visual assets

Technology brand icons use assets from the
[Simple Icons](https://simpleicons.org/) project, either as local SVG files in
`public/images/technology/` or through its local Iconify collection.

Brand names and trademarks remain the property of their respective owners. The
icons are used only to identify technologies applied in the work described by
this website and their shapes have not been modified.
